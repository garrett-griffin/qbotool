Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-09

This application is a private, single-user tool used solely by its owner to access the owner’s own QuickBooks Online account.

Information Collected

  • QuickBooks Online data: The app accesses the owner’s QuickBooks Online data via Intuit OAuth and APIs.
  • OAuth tokens: Access/refresh tokens may be stored locally on the owner’s machine to maintain access.

How Data Is Used

  • To authenticate with Intuit using OAuth and perform actions requested by the owner.
  • To read and/or update the owner’s accounting data within the owner’s QuickBooks Online company.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties, sold, or transmitted to any external service beyond Intuit’s QuickBooks Online platform, except as required to authenticate and make API calls to Intuit.

Data Storage

Data is not stored outside the local machine, except as it exists within the owner’s QuickBooks Online account. OAuth tokens are stored locally and securely by the owner.

Security

The owner is responsible for securing their local environment. The app is intended to run only in the owner’s controlled environment.
